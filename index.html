G20 Countries - Healthcare, Education and Military Spending

This project is about analyzing the spends made by G20 countries on healthcare, education
and military, comparing these spends to the GDP for each country. These indicators give us important
insights into each country's policies regarding these sectors and their social welfare. For this study
we have considered data from 2012 - 2017.

For the purposes of this study, we have selected ten countries from the G20 and
